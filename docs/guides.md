# Guides & Helpful Links

This page is a work in progress! If you'd like some information of yours to be included, or want to contribute a brand-new guide or resource, please let us know!

## Guides
The following links include both general GPosing tips and Ktisis-specific information. Oftentimes too, understanding the principles of IRL photography and scene composition can help you make and take better pictures in-game!

- [Tips for DT Face Bone Posing](https://xivmodding.com/books/general-gposing/page/tips-for-dt-face-bone-posing) by Nya & StrayCatte_XIV - detailing the different sets of bones made available with Dawntrail, plus how & when to use them
- [Basics of Lighting](https://xivmodding.com/books/general-gposing/page/basics-of-lighting) by Nya - covering the vanilla lighiting controls that are available during GPose
- [Ktisis Lighting Quick Guide](https://bsky.app/profile/leatheruptime.bsky.social/post/3lpqhbxtnrc2y) by Uros - guiding principles for how to light a scene using Ktisis' different light settings
- [Ktisis Lights + Portrait Gpose Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=70j2YKGNyq4) by Snow - an exhaustive walk-through of Snow's process for posing and lighting portraits & headshots using Ktisis, Brio, and ReShade

## Resources
The following are links to useful plugins, tools, and Discords for GPosing and working with FFXIV mods.

- [Sea of Stars Plugins](https://github.com/Ottermandias/SeaOfStars#sea-of-stars) - one central download link to all the 3rd-party-plugins you could need, including Ktisis!
- [TimelineAnimator](https://github.com/NoHideout/TimelineAnimator) - built by Leyla, this plugin integrates with Ktisis to let you keyframe and make custom animations using GPose!
- [GPosingway](https://github.com/gposingway/gposingway/blob/main/md/installation_guide.md) - an easy install process to add ReShade and its shaders/presets support to XIV
- [The Reniguide](https://reniguide.carrd.co/) by Reni - offering a step-by-step process to get started with Penumbra, Glamourer, and other common appearance mods
- [Aetherworks Discord](https://discord.gg/KvGJCCnG8t) - the home server for Anamnesis, Customize+, and Brio development along with general screenshot and GPose info
- [GPOSERS Discord](https://discord.com/invite/gposers) - the main hub for over 40,000 GPose enthusiastis!
- [Gposing Central](https://discord.com/invite/nk6AzDnVGD) - Leyla's server for GPosing and support on the TimelineAnimator plugin
- [XIVLauncher & Dalamud Discord](https://goat.place/) - support or discussion of 3rd-party-plugins is not allowed, but it's the place to go for any info on Dalamud itself and how to get started with XIVLauncher
