## About Ktisis
[Ktisis](https://github.com/ktisis-tools/ktisis) is a robust posing tool that enhances the capabilities of FFXIV's GPose mode. It follows in the footsteps of [CMTool](https://github.com/imchillin/CMTool) and [Anamnesis](https://github.com/imchillin/Anamnesis) to allow users to pose characters with control over individual bones, while also offering a full suite of controls over other aspects of photo or video scene composition.

???+ info "Standard features include:"

    - Manipulate poses using any bones on a character's skeleton
    - Customize their appearances, animations, and expressions
    - Spawn as many light sources as you need, with settings beyond vanilla limits
    - Save and load `.chara` and `.pose` files compatible with other posing tools
    - Adding custom dialog, buff & debuff overlays using vanilla UI elements

??? info "Advanced controls including, but not limited to:"

    - Add and pose over 200 actors at once (compatible with other plugins and MCDF data!)
    - Undo, redo, flip poses, or load vanilla face expressions with ease
    - Creating custom expressions through the use of FACS sliders, rather than bone manipulation
    - More easily position arms, legs, and tails using Inverse Kimenatics in and out of posing
    - Weather, time of day, and broader environment editing
    - Manipulating objects and lights from the overworld within GPose
